Ajax Wannabes

We were founded in 2015 by Wayne Young. We are group of friends that enjoys each others company. We Swim, we bike, and run and we never left anybody behind. The Ajax Wannabes athletic community began years ago with like-minded individuals chasing a Boston Marathon dream. We have grown to include all levels of ability and interest. We range from novice to boston marathon qualifier to elite level.

  • Social, inclusive atmosphere
  • Experienced athletes
  • Year-round training and events
  • Cool gear
  • Free to join
